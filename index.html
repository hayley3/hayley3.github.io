Meet Makenna!
Founder of Ken's Kicks

Hello! My name is MaKenna McGough and I am an undergraduate student at UNC Chapel Hill. I first started custom painting shoes, helmets, and water bottles for family and friends over quarantine last March. Now, Kens Kicks has allowed me to combine my passion for art and nutrition by donating 10% of the profits I make to NEDA. My goal is for people to enjoy their custom painted items while also helping to raise awareness around eating disorders, especially in the college realm.
